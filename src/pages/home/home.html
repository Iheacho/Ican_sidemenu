Welcome

It gives me immense pleasure to heartily welcome all our distinguished delegates to the 47th Annual Accountants Conference of the Institute of Chartered Accountants taking place at the International Conference Centre Abuja, on Monday to Friday, 16 - 20 October 2017, under the theme “Nigeria First: Accountancy, Nation Building and the Future”.

This landmark event prides itself as the largest congregation of chartered accountants in Africa. Last year, about 4,000 delegates comprising Chartered Accountants in public practice, commerce and industry, public sector, the academia as well as leaders of professional accounting organisations from across the West African sub-region attended the conference.

Every year, the ICAN Annual Accountants’ Conference has traditionally combined great professional content with a tremendous opportunity to learn, exchange views, play, compete, relax, laugh and enjoy the camaraderie of friends and professional colleagues. This year’s conference has been designed not only to educate, enrich, excite, recharge and reinvigorate delegates but also to exceedingly supersede the usual high standards that our members have come to expect. I hope you enjoy every minute of it!

An outstanding gallery of world-class and sought after speakers will participate and share with us the most significant advances in our profession. Whatever sessions you choose to attend, they all seek to expand the profession’s horizons by empowering delegates with the latest knowledge and tools to become future relevant Chartered Accountants, while upholding the core principles of integrity, accountability and trust which have made our profession invaluable and indispensable.

Of course I must thank each of our world-class speakers. All of them experts and leaders in their fields for graciously agreeing to share their wisdom and experience with us, and for this, we are eternally grateful.

One unique feature of this year’s conference is its focus on the role of Chartered Accountants in Nation Building leveraging on our public interest mandate. The Institute will be unveiling and launching of the ICAN Accountability Index which is the first of its kind mechanism for assessing public finance management and public governance practices of Nigerian public sector institutions including the Executive (Federal, State and Local Governments), the Legislature and the Judiciary.

The Institute is very proud of its role and that of other stakeholders in the development of this index, which we believe is a relevant, innovative and timely contribution to Accountability, Public Finance Management and Governance practices in Nigeria.

We have a great team of members and staff on the Annual Accountants Conference Committee working tirelessly to make this year’s conference a truly memorable event. I hope that you will join me in expressing thanks to them over the next few days, wherever you meet them, for their time, outstanding ideas, dedication and selfless service committed towards organising this conference.

Finally, I thank all our distinguished delegates for choosing to attend and participate at the 47th Annual Accountants’ Conference and happily look forward to meeting you.



GOD BLESS YOU

Isma'ila M. Zakari mni, FBR, FCA
